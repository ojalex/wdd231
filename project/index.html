Hope 4 The Youth


Hope 4 The Youth Kenya: is a youth-led community organization focused on promoting youth health and development. It uses advocacy, technical assistance, and capacity building to improve access to quality, affordable health services and social accountability. Partnering with like-minded organizations, it advocates for youth-responsive policies and programs in Siaya County through mentorship and capacity development.t.

Our Focus is;

  1. To create a platform for young people to interact, learn, and acquire skills for societal success.
  2. To build effective linkages for economic empowerment to enhance self-reliance and sustainability.
  3. To promote and advocate for the rights of youth, women, and girls through mentorship and meaningful participation opportunities.

Vision Statement: Healthy and Sustainable Communities.

Mission Statement: To promote the development of Healthy and Sustainable communities through Partnerships, Advocacy, and Empowerment.

Our Core Values

  1. Equity
  2. Inclusion
  3. Empowerment
  4. Leadership Development
  5. Resilience
  6. Collaboration
